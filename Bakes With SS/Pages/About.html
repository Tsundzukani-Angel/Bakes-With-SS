About Us

Bakes With SS was founded in 2025 by a student, Karabo Shingange. It sarted off as a home-based side hustle baking birthday cakes for friends and family members. The small side hustle turned into a thriving business through word of mouth and social media platforms.

Our Mission

Our mission is to craft beautiful, delicious and unforgettable cakes that brings joy to every celebration

Our Vission

We want to become Limpopo's most loved custom cake baker, known for creativity, consistency and exceptional customer experience, both in person and online.
