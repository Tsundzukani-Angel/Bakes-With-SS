Bakes With SS was founded in 2025 by a student, Karabo Shingange. It sarted off as a home-based side hustle baking birthday cakes for friends and family members. The small side hustle turned into a thriving business through word of mouth and social media platforms.

My mission is to craft beautiful, delicious and unforgettable cakes that brings joy to every celebration, and is that i want to become Limpopo's most loved custom cake baker, known for creativity, consistency and exceptional customer experience, both in person and online.
